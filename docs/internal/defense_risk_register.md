## Highest-Risk Defense Themes

1. **Causality wording around detectors:** always separate causal `rv_baseline` from auxiliary/offline `rv_dwell`.
2. **Mechanism overclaiming:** signal redundancy is well supported; categorical-channel interference is descriptive, not proven mechanism.
3. **Synthetic-market scope:** avoid implying live-market deployment or real-order-book validation.
4. **TOST explanation:** be ready to explain why equivalence testing is stronger than "p>0.05".

## Recommended Opening Defense Position

The thesis should be defended as a controlled synthetic-market study of information design in PPO market making. Its contribution is not that regime labels are useless everywhere; it is that, in this environment and observation design, explicit labels do not add reliable value beyond `sigma_hat`, and this finding survives detector robustness, oracle labels, reward-shaping, misspecification, and WP6 signal-degradation tests.
