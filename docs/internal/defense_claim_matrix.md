Reproducibility labels:

- **Directly reproducible:** can be recomputed from existing frozen CSVs with active scripts, without retraining PPO.
- **Indirectly reproducible:** the pipeline/config/code exist, but full reproduction requires expensive experiment reruns or PPO retraining.
- **Historical/protected artifact only:** artifact is intentionally frozen/protected for audit; do not regenerate for defense.

Support labels:

- **Statistically supported:** backed by formal tests, CIs, p-values, or protected statistical summaries.
- **Descriptive only:** empirical pattern or figure/table summary without a formal inferential claim.
- **Interpretive only:** thesis-level explanation consistent with evidence but not itself a directly tested mechanism.

Every major thesis claim is traceable to a named artifact, script, config, and run family. The only claims that should be defended as mechanisms are the simulator/pipeline mechanics themselves; signal redundancy and categorical-channel degradation should be defended as evidence-backed interpretations.
